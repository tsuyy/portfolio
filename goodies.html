How might we promote sustainable lifestyle using a digital platform?

OVERVIEW

One way to live more sustainably is reducing the food miles of the products we consume.
The easiest way to do that would be buying local and seasonal produce rather than imported ones. This app is designed to streamline the process of discovering what's in season and where to buy those goodies based on the user's location.


KEY FEATURES

Search for a specific fruit, vegetable or a local farmer's market.
Calendar dispalying whatever is in season based on the user's location.
Produce info displaying the nutritional benefits, how to choose and store and the seasonality of the produce.
