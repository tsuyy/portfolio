OVERVIEW

The Evolution of Ski Boots was a project I developed in the courses of 3D Design and Advanced Data Visualization at the University of Miami. The main objective of the classes were to create a 3D model using Autodesk Maya and design a browser based interactive visualization. This project explores how the design of ski boots have progressed over the years and its possible causal effects on various leg injuries.
